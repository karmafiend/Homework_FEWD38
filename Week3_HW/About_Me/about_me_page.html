Chris Kilkes

"Isn't life a series of images that change as they repeat themselves?" - Andy Warhol

Some biographical information:

  • Born in Hollywood
  • Grew up in Burbank, the real movie capital of America
  • Went to school at UCLA after dreaming of doing so since the age of five
  • Moved to SF to go to grad school at SF State to become a history teacher
  • Dropped out during the first dot com boom to work at one of the first digital ad agencies
  • Been working for the man since then while painting and taking pictures the entire time
  • First started painting 18 years ago when I thought that any idiot could paint like Jackson Pollock
  • This idiot learned quickly that that is definitely not true
  • Really became obsessed with photography in the last year and a half since first shooting in full manual mode
  • Manual mode really makes me happy

What equipment do I use to shoot pictures?

  • Sony A7II Full Frame Mirrorless Camera
  • Sony FE 24-240mm F3.5-5.6 Zoom Lense
  • Sony FE 35mm F2.8 Prime Lense
  • And that's it...for now!
